Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my daily meals?
To track your daily meals, simply log in to your account, click on "Add Meal" button, and enter the food items you've consumed. The system will automatically calculate the nutritional values for you. You can track breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks separately.
Can I set nutritional goals?
Yes! Nuttrify allows you to set daily nutritional goals for calories, protein, carbs, and fats. Navigate to your profile settings and select "Goals" to customize them according to your dietary needs and personal health objectives.
How accurate is the nutritional information?
Our nutritional database contains information from verified sources and is regularly updated. While we strive for accuracy, there may be slight variations compared to package labels due to different serving sizes or preparation methods. For packaged foods, you can also scan barcodes for precise information.
Can I export my meal history?
Yes, you can export your meal history as a CSV or PDF file. Go to your profile, select "Meal History," choose the date range you want to export, and click on the "Export" button. This feature is great for sharing information with nutritionists or healthcare providers.
